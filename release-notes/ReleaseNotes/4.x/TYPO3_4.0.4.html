RELEASE NOTES FOR TYPO3 4.0.4

This document describes all the important information for the TYPO3 release 4.0.4 that where not mentioned in the official article on typo3.org

(this link will work in january: https://typo3.org/documentation/article/release-notes-404/)


in simple words:

  • Fixed security-problem - when a website uses the rich-text-editor at the front-end
  • Fixed small bugs of the versioning-module
  • Fixed email-subscription-problem of Forum tt_board
